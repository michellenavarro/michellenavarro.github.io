01 — About

Engineer.
Cartographer-
in-training.
Angeleno.

I'm a full-stack engineer with 6+ years shipping production systems — event-driven data pipelines, client-facing REST APIs, LLM-powered products, and the observability infrastructure that keeps them honest. My core stack is Java and Spring Boot, with React and Node on the frontend side.

In late 2023 I made a deliberate decision to step back from engineering and figure out what I actually wanted my work to be about. I'd spent years building ad tech at scale and I was good at it — but I wanted my skills to matter differently.

That time led me somewhere unexpected. I reconnected with the physical world — land, water, weather, the geography underneath the data. I started a GIS certificate at Pasadena City College and enrolled in environmental data science coursework through the University of Edinburgh. Not as a resume move. Because maps are one of the most powerful ways we make sense of a world that's changing fast, and I want to be part of that.

I'm based in Los Angeles. I run (finished my first marathon in 2025). I hike whenever I can escape the city. I'm focused on roles in climate tech and clean energy where what I know how to build meets work that actually matters.
