About

I'm a full-stack engineer with 6+ years shipping production-grade systems — data pipelines, analytics platforms, client-facing APIs, and LLM-powered products.

I spent most of my career in ad tech at Taboola, building things at scale with Java, Spring Boot, React, and Airflow. I was good at it. And in late 2023, I made a deliberate decision to step back and figure out what I actually wanted my work to be about.

That time led me somewhere I didn't expect. I reconnected with the natural world — maps, land, energy systems, the physical infrastructure underneath the data. I started pursuing a GIS certificate at Pasadena City College and enrolled in environmental data science coursework. Not as a resume move. Because I genuinely care about what happens to the planet, and I want to build things that help.

Now I'm focused on applying the engineering skills I've built over six years to climate tech, clean energy, and environmental data. That's where I'm headed, and I'm not hedging about it.
